Our Story

Where Nature Meets
Tradition

Nestled in the lush Western Ghats of Kerala, Uruliyanickal Cardamom Plantation is a family-owned estate devoted to cultivating the finest green cardamom — the Queen of Spices.

Our plantation thrives in the cool, misty highlands where elevation, rainfall, and rich forest soil combine to produce cardamom pods of exceptional aroma, flavour, and quality. Every pod is hand-picked at peak ripeness.

We follow sustainable, eco-friendly farming practices under a natural forest canopy — preserving biodiversity while delivering premium spice to markets across India and beyond.
